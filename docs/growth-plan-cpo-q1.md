# Growth plan — CPO Q1 (the 5 ideas, parked)

> _Five behavior-design loops that, in sequence, produce: insane individual aha · same-day-hour return · viral effect (k > 0.5)._
>
> _**Status:** approved as ideas; deferred as the *active* sequence. The active priority bet is in [`aha-and-network.md`](./aha-and-network.md), which keeps the two strongest of these five (Cold-Start Aha, Spark Cards) and adds 8 sharper individual-wow + content + FTUE items. Cohort Quests + the bigger Build Loop come back here once the individual-wow sequence ships and we have data to evaluate them on._

---

## The 5 ideas, ranked

| # | Idea | Aha | Same-day hour | Viral |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Cold-Start Aha** — 60-second AI interview at sign-up that drafts 3 Sparks tuned to you, by name | ⭐⭐⭐ | ⭐ | ⭐⭐ |
| 2 | **Spark Cards** — every 👍'd Spark becomes a Wordle-style shareable card | ⭐ | ⭐ | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| 3 | **The Daily Pulse** — 60-second personalized morning brief (push + email + Home tile) | ⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐⭐ | ⭐ |
| 4 | **Build-and-Ship Loop with Public Build Profile** — proof of work that LinkedIn can't deliver | ⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| 5 | **7-Day Cohort Quests** — multi-day, multi-builder, matched cohort, async + live | ⭐ | ⭐⭐⭐ | ⭐⭐ |

**Why this set.** Each is a **behavior-design loop**, not a feature. Hooked-style Trigger → Action → Reward → Investment circuits; BJ Fogg Motivation × Ability × Trigger; each ladders to ≥ 1 KPI in [`metrics.md`](./metrics.md). They are **multiplicative**, not additive — Cohort Quests without aha don't form cohorts; Pulse without Cold-Start is generic.

**Why deferring some.** Per the operator's 2026-05 directive: focus 90% on individual wow first, 10% on network. Cohort Quests (#5) and the bigger Build-and-Ship Loop (#4) are network-heavy bets that need an aha foundation underneath. We ship that foundation in [`aha-and-network.md`](./aha-and-network.md) first, then evaluate when these come back to the top.
